# Bloom (Personal) — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 6, 2026_

Bloom is a personal-use, non-commercial health coaching application operated by a single individual for their own use. It is not offered to the public.

## Data collected
With your authorization via WHOOP's OAuth flow, the app reads WHOOP data including recovery scores, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, strain, sleep, workouts, profile, and body measurements.

## How data is used
Data is used solely to provide personalized physical-activity coaching to the app's owner (who is also its only user). It is stored in a private Firebase/Firestore database controlled by the owner.

## Sharing
Data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties. Portions of the data may be processed by an LLM inference provider (e.g., OpenAI) strictly to generate coaching responses.

## Retention & deletion
Data is retained only as long as useful for coaching. Access can be revoked at any time from the WHOOP app (Settings → Connected Apps), and stored data can be deleted by the owner on request to the contact below.
